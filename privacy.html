Reinhart Buenk

2. Information Collected

I collect the following personal information:

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

Your information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

The following third-party services may process your personal information as part of delivering the service:

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy. I recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns about how they handle your data.

5. Data Retention

Personal information is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with any legal or tax obligations. Inactive records are deleted within three years of the last interaction unless retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information I hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to any applicable legal requirements.
  • Object to the processing of your information.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, email rbuenk33@gmail.com.

Refund Policy

This policy applies to all mentorship sessions and programmes booked and paid for through this website.

1-on-1 Sessions

Sessions are booked and paid in advance via Paystack.

  • Cancel more than 24 hours before your session: full refund.
  • Cancel within 24 hours of your session: no refund.
  • No-show (session time passes without notice): no refund.

Structured Programmes

  • Cancel before the programme begins: full refund.
  • Cancel after the first session: 50% refund on the remaining balance.
  • Cancel after two or more sessions: no refund.

Cancellations by Me

If I need to cancel or reschedule a session, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule at no extra cost.

How to Request a Refund

Email rbuenk33@gmail.com with your name and booking reference. Refunds are processed as quickly as possible, typically within 5 to 10 business days, depending on Paystack and your bank, and are returned to the original payment method.
